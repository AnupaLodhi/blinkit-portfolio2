# blinkit-portfolio2
📌 Project Overview
This is a growth marketing case study designed to solve one of the most common challenges in quick commerce — converting app installs into actual paying customers.
Despite Blinkit's strong brand presence and high app install rates, a large segment of users — especially students and young professionals aged 18–28 — were downloading the app but never placing a first order.
This project builds a complete end-to-end growth strategy that bridges the gap between user intent and action using situation-based marketing, campus outreach, and conversion funnel optimization.

Core Positioning: "Blinkit — Your solution to everyday emergencies."

❗ Problem Statement
Although Blinkit had high app installs, the following challenges led to poor conversion:

Low first-order conversion rate — users explored but didn't buy
High price sensitivity — especially among the student segment
No urgency triggers — users only ordered when they had an immediate need
Checkout drop-offs — friction in the final steps caused last-minute abandonment


The core problem: Converting intent into action.


👤 Target Audience

Age: 18–28 years
Segment: College students & young working professionals
Cities: Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru (Tier-1)
Platforms: Instagram, YouTube Shorts, Snapchat
Behaviour: Price-conscious, impulse buyers, late-night shoppers

💡 Strategy
Situation-Based Marketing (Not Feature Marketing)
Instead of promoting "10-minute delivery", the campaign highlighted emotionally relatable moments:
SituationMessage Angle🌙 Midnight cravings"It's 2AM and the hunger hits. Blinkit's got you."🏃 Last-minute grocery run"Guests in 10 mins? Blinkit in 10 mins."👨‍👩‍👧 Unexpected guests"Empty fridge. Full guest list. Blinkit."📚 Study-night essentials"Can't leave the library. Blinkit can."

🚀 Implementation
7.1 Short-Form Content Marketing

Created 40+ Instagram Reels based on relatable real-life situations
Strong 2-second hooks to stop mid-scroll
Clear CTA: "Order now on Blinkit"
A/B tested hooks across audience segments

7.2 Campus Ambassador Program

Collaborated with 20 college micro-influencers
Unique referral codes per ambassador for tracking
₹100 first-order discount for new users
Limited-time campus challenges for organic virality
Performance tracked via Mixpanel & Firebase

7.3 Funnel Optimization

Highlighted first-order discounts on homepage banner
Reduced checkout steps to minimize friction
Added exit-intent offer pop-ups at drop-off points
Sent push notification reminders within 24 hours of install
